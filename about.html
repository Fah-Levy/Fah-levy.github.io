ME

I write to weave simple words that can leave a trace in the heart.
Poetry and quotes are my way of speaking, even in silence.
Beyond writing, I share the journey of small projects I’m building.
For me, creation is a diary of life — a place to learn, fail, and try again.
I believe small things can carry meaning, as long as they are made with honesty.
This is my space to share stories, process, and a little light from the journey.
